Service Description

What is Cloud Connect?

vBridge Cloud Connect is a Veeam compatible offsite storage location for the retention of Veeam backup files.

You must already have a compatible and licenced version of Veeam 9.5 (Backup and Recovery) on your premises in order to use Cloud Connect. vBridge does not provide support for Veeam on a customer premises.

You will be allocated and billed for two quotas for use of the service:

  1. Number of virtual machines to backup.
  2. Total size (in GB) of the committed repository for backup storage environment.

The quotas are allocated per service and are limited by availability zone. For example, you have a Cloud Connect service in Christchurch with 2000GB quota. To use Cloud Connect in Auckland you will require a separate service. We will retain one copy of the data deposited in the Cloud Connect repository only.

Ingress and egress bandwidth for your Cloud Connect service within the vBridge data centre is included in the service price point. We reserve the right to throttle bandwidth.

Roles and Responsibilities

vBridge engineers are generally responsible for the service provider side or the Veeam Cloud Connect environment.  This includes the physical equipment, the operating systems on that equipment, and the configuration and management of the service provider side of the Veeam Cloud Connect platform. The customer or reseller team is responsible for the management of the on-premise software (Veeam B&R or Veeam Agent), backups, restores, schedules and retention policies.

Responsibilities of the service provider (vBridge):

  • Monitor resources and provide tuning and advisement for Veeam Cloud Connect service provider issues
  • Engineer all layers of the Veeam technology stack for high availability and compliance to vBridge policy
  • Apply patches and upgrades as recommended for the Cloud Connect infrastructure
  • Monitor and tune the physical server and storage environment as needed
  • Manage permissions and security groups within the Cloud Connect infrastructure
  • Coordinate with vendors for licensing, maintenance and support requests
  • Perform medium-term capacity planning for the Cloud Connect infrastructure

Cloud Connect endpoints (Veeam Backup and Replication or Veeam agents) may be managed by the customer, or may be contracted to a vBridge reseller

In either case, the responsibilities of the endpoint administrator are:

  • Serve as the primary trouble-shooter for issues with a Veeam endpoint
  • Maintaining and managing the client side Veeam environment. This includes the physical equipment, the operating systems on that equipment, the software, licensing and the configuration of the client side
  • Manage all backup schedules and retention policies
  • Manage and maintain all client-side encryption keys
  • Monitor all backup jobs for completion and errors as required
  • Manage all restores and DR testing onto on-premise environment
  • Ensure that all client-side software is correctly licensed and reported

Optional Services

vBridge are able to restore customer Cloud Connect images taken of VMware hosted servers onto the vBridge IaaS platform under certain circumstances. Any restoration or creation of IaaS resources in a DR or recovery situation is done on a time and materials and best effort basis.

For seeding of data, in some circumstances, the initial transfer of data may be too much for client Internet connectivity. Data may be seeded to vBridge in a variety of ways, including SFTP and USB drive drop off. Time and materials charges may apply.

Accessing Service

Accessing the Service

This service can be provisioned and managed through the MyCloudSpace portal, or provisioned on behalf of the customer by vBridge staff.

The services endpoints for vBridge Cloud Connect are:

  • Christchurch: cloudconnect.vbridge.co.nz port 6180
  • Auckland: auckland.mycloudbackup.co.nz port 6180

Note: These FQDNs point to load balanced gateway clusters.  The IP addresses behind these may change without notice.

To configure your Veeam Availability Suite or Veeam Backup console, please refer to vendor documentation from Veeam.

Contract

Data Retention Upon Cancellation

Upon cancellation of the service, Cloud Connect data will be deleted and will no longer be available. vBridge will use reasonable effort to assist in the transition of data back to you.

Cloud Connect services have a minimum contract term of 1 month.
