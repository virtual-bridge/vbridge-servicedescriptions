What is Cloud Backup for Office 365?

Microsoft Office 365 is the largest solution for digital collaboration today. Office 365 customers can choose to connect to their on-premises workloads and benefit from a seamless user experience — even when moving between different environments — creating a hybrid platform. Office 365 has a high adoption rate by businesses of all sizes.

While Office 365 offers an undisputed flexibility with the integration of several solutions, it deeply focuses on the infrastructure management rather than data management. The responsibility for data management and its Availability still belongs to the respective owners leveraging the Office 365 services.

In fact, Office 365 data centre uptime refers to the infrastructure rather than data hosted in the data centres. vBridge Cloud Backup for Office 365 is a Veeam compatible offsite storage location for the retention of Microsoft Office 365 data and files (Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, OneDrive) which addresses the below scenarios:

  • Accidental deletions
  • Data loss / corruption
  • Rogue apps / insider threats
  • Outages

You must already have a Microsoft Office subscription in order to use Cloud Backup for Office 365. vBridge does not provide support for Microsoft Office.

Overview

Customer Profile

This service is targeted to organisations of all sizes that require protection from or adherence to the below scenarios:

  • Accidental deletions
  • Retention policy gaps and confusion
  • Internal security threats
  • External security threats
  • Legal compliance requirements

The use of vBridge Cloud Backup for Office 365 also results in a copy of 0365 data being physically located within New Zealand to satisfy any data sovereignty concerns.

You will be allocated and billed for two quotas for use of the service:

1) Number of users included in the backup.

2) Total size (in GB) of the committed data within the backup storage environment, billed for and calculated on an hourly basis

We will retain one copy of the data deposited in the Cloud Backup for O365 repository only.

Ingress and egress bandwidth for your service within the vBridge data centre is included in the service price point. We reserve the right to throttle bandwidth.

Roles and Responsibilities

vBridge engineers are generally responsible for the service provider side or the Cloud Backup for Office 365 environment. This includes the physical equipment, the operating systems on that equipment, and the configuration and management of the service provider side of the Cloud Backup for Office 365 platform. The customer or reseller team is responsible for the management of the on-premise software (Veeam B&R or Veeam Agent), Microsoft Office administration, support and subscription management.

Responsibilities of the service provider (vBridge):

  • Monitor resources and provide tuning and advisement for Cloud Backup for Office 365 service provider issues
  • Engineer all layers of the Veeam technology stack for high availability and compliance to vBridge policy
  • Apply patches and upgrades as recommended for the Cloud Backup for Office 365 infrastructure
  • Monitor and tune the physical server and storage environment as needed
  • Manage permissions and security groups within the Cloud Backup for Office 365 infrastructure
  • Coordinate with vendors for licensing, maintenance and support requests
  • Perform medium-term capacity planning for the Cloud Backup for Office 365 infrastructure

Office 365 tenancies may be managed by the customer, or may be contracted to a vBridge reseller

In either case, the responsibilities of the endpoint administrator are:

  • Serve as the primary trouble-shooter for issues with Office 365
  • Maintaining and managing the client-side environment. This includes the physical equipment, the operating systems on that equipment, the software, licensing and the configuration of the client side
  • Manage all retention policies and any specified exclusions on jobs
  • Monitor all backup jobs for completion and errors as required
  • Manage all restores
  • Ensure that all client-side software is correctly licensed and reported
